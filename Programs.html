Programs

Our programs are grouped in three thematic areas. They are:
1. Business Development Support (BDS)
2. Micro Enterprise Park (MEP), and
3. Socio-Economic Development Intervention Programs (SEDIP).
We ensure that beneficiaries are involved in our program design, from research to implementation. This participatory approach helps to tailor our programs to address their needs. Learn More
