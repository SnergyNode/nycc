Life is driven by purpose. I found my purpose and fulfilment in working with young people. And for young people.

Young people are critical assets for socio-economic development and represent the hope of the Nation only to the extent to which their demographic advantages are deliberately harnessed, positively engaged and channeled toward productive contribution to Nation building.

I am inspired by the potentials of young people which find expressions in their raw energies, creative ingenuity, and innovative inclinations. This stimulates in me a deep desire to assist in nurturing them to unleash their capabilities productively.

It is clear young people are faced with two critical challenges bordering on social and economic deprivations. I am commitment to continuously identify these gaps and drive outcome-oriented intervention programmes that can help address them sustainably.

The Problem

Over 99% of the 45 million enterprises operating in Nigeria are micro in nature. Evidence shows that these microenterprises contribute over 65% to the GDP of the economy. They also account for 70% of the employed workforce in the country. No doubt, they are the engine room of the economy. However, SMEs operate largely in the informal sector. As a result, they are unorganized. MSMEs in Nigeria lack access to enablers of business growth and development. They lack access to finance, endure gross infrastructure deficit, and experience weak systemic support. Clearly, these enterprises cannot compete in any market on the globe.

Enter NYCC

This is exactly why the Nigerian Youth Chamber of Commerce (NYCC) was launched in 2010. The organization limited by guarantee was registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in 2014. The Chamber is a Business Membership organization for aspiring, startup and existing MSMEs in Nigeria. NYCC serves as the organized private sector platform for enterprises that operate in the informal sector. The Chamber leverages this social capital to organize self-help and create innovative solutions that drive sustainable business development.
