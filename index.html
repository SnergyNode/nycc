Lets take your Business to Next Level

Nigerian Youth Chamber of Commerce (NYCC) is a Business Membership organization for aspiring, startup and existing MSMEs in Nigeria. NYCC serves as the organized private sector platform for enterprises that operate in the informal sector. The Chamber leverages this social capital to organize self-help and create innovative solutions that drive sustainable business development.
