National Vocational Skills Development Program (NVSDP)

Skills for the bedrock of every country’s economy. They are not only linked to aggregate economic performance but also to everyone’s success in the labour market. The recently published ITF and UNIDO Skills Gap Assessment Report acknowledges skills capacity deficit in most of the sectors of the economy.

The NVSDP is developed to address the skills gap challenge in the economy. The program will skill up young people who seek a vocational career. Assess and administer Competency Based Education and Training (CBET) for artisans. This will stimulate the supply side of skilled and competent workforce in the country.
