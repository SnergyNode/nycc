Membership

NYCC has over 5,000 members drawn from 31 states of Nigeria. Kebbi, Zamfara, Yobe, Borno and Benue states will have our presence by the second quarter of 2020. The chamber is a proud Member of the Pan African Youth Chamber of Commerce and the World Youth Chamber of Commerce.

Membership Categories

Individual Membership:

An individual member is any youth entrepreneur who is already in business or willing to start a business.

Organizational Membership:

An organizational member is a duly registered youth enterprise organization, Cooperative or Business Association.

Corporate Membership:

A Corporate member is any organization that believes in the vision, mission and objectives of the chamber and is willing to be an active member of chamber.

Partner Membership:

Partner members are honorary Institutions and Corporate Organizations who passionately believe in and support the course of youth entrepreneurship and nation building.

Student Membership:

Student members are student of primary, secondary and tertiary institutions interested in becoming small business owners now or in the future.

Benefits of Membership

Though, membership of the NYCC is voluntary, benefits of being a member of NYCC are numerous.


1. Enjoy the prestige of belonging to a recognized, well organized, and result oriented youth trade and commerce platform.
2. Gain access to high level networking with peers, net worth individuals, relevant institutions to facilitate trade relations.
3. Enjoy entrepreneurship trainings and business development support to grow your business to profitability.
4. Subsidized access to innovative tools that help you improve business operations.
5. Participate in economic development intervention programmes and opportunities for growth.
6. Be the first among equals to gain access to business information and trade opportunities.
7. Enjoy easy access to regulatory and policy institutions to complete business processes.
8. Participate in local and international trade fairs and product exhibitions.
9. Participate in local and international cooperation, trade missions and exchange Programmes.

How to become a member of NYCC

In three simple steps, you can join the chamber and leapfrog the journey towards success for your business.


1. Fill and return membership form along with a recent passport size photograph and two referees.

2. Pay a non-refundable membership subscription fee of:
a. Individual Membership - N10,000.00
b. Affiliate Membership - N20,000.00
c. Corporate Membership - N50,000.00
d. Student Membership - N1,000.00

3. Pay annual subscription fee of:
e. Individual Membership - N10,000.00
f. Affiliate Membership - N30,000.00
g. Corporate Membership - N100,000.00
h. Student Membership - N4,000.00

All membership fees and subscriptions are subject to review without prior notice.
