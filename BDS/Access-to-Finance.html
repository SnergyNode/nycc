Access to Finance

The chamber is appointed by NIRSAL MFB as Entrepreneurial Development Institute (EDI). We have a mandate to deliver entrepreneurship training to applicants of the CBN AGSMEIS - an Enterprises Investment Scheme of the Central Bank of Nigeria. Our commitment is to help 70% of our trainees to access AGSMEIS. To achieve this target, we support business plan development and facilitate loan procurement.
